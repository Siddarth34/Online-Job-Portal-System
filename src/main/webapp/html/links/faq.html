Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I apply for a job?

Search for a job using the search bar and click on the "Apply" button next to your desired job listing.

2. Is it free to use the portal?

Yes, it is completely free for job seekers and companies to register and use basic features.

3. How can I contact the company?

Use the "Contact Us" page to get in touch. You can also email us directly if needed.

4. How can I update my profile or resume?

You can update your profile and resume at any time by logging into your account. Go to your dashboard and click on the 'Edit Profile' section, where you can update your personal details, resume, and other information.
